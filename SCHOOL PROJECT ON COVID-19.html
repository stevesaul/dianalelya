Covid-19

The corona virus or covid-19 is a communicable respiratory disease caused by a new strain of coronavirus that causes illness in humans.
Covid-19 was first reported in Wuhan China, but now it has spread throughout the world.


HOW IS IT

SPREAD?

  • The disease spreads from one person to another though infected air droplets that are projected during coughing or sneezing
  • It can also be transmitted when humans have contact with hands or surfaces that contain the virus and touch their eyes,
    nose, or mouth     with the contaminated hands.

However there are ways to ensure you do not get this virus. A few are:

  1. Washing your hands frequently.
  2. Not touching your face.
  3. Always cover your mouth when you cough or sneaze.
  4. Staying home.
  5. Follow the governments directives.


Effects of the virus.

Without a doubt this pandemic has affected our daily lives in very many different ways.
First off, it is now unsafe to leave the house to meet others, especially at public gatherings.
A few examples of those public gatherings are:

  • Weddings:-
    Many people have not been able to attend their loved ones weddings seeing as it is now a health hazard to have a gathering of more than 20 people.
    The ceremony now only includes the bride, the groom and each of their parents.

    • A small wedding ceremony happening during the pandemic.

  • Funerals:-
    Families all over the world cannot bury the loved ones they lost properly since the ceremonies must now become much more brief and are attended by much less people so as to reduce the risk of contracting the virus.
  • Church services:-
    Churches have been closed for the first time since nearly 1918, when the spanish flu put the world at a standstill.

    • The citam chaurch online service advertisement.

  • School:-
    • schools have also had to be shut down seeing as it would be nearly impossible to continue to maintain social distancing at such a large gathering.
    Ever since the disease found its way to Kenya classes have been forced to move online. It has been about five months since the new classes began.
    This has been a new experience and a very huge shift to go from normal, physical, school to the virtual online classes.
